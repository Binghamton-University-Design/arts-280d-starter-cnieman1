About me

Chloe Niemann is a passionate and creative mind.

Chloe enjoys art, design, and photography, as well as costume and fashion design, creating stuffed animals, jewelry, and anything else messy (that she is allowed to do in her parents' NYC apartment!).

She also has a passion for politics, the environment (she has way too many houseplants), literature, and community organizing.

This website is a space where she is excited to showcase her work!
